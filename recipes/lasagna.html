Lasagna

Description

A baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pasta

Steps in making Lasagna

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce
  3. Combine Cheese Mixture
  4. Layer meat sauce and cheese mixture and bake
  5. Enjoy!
